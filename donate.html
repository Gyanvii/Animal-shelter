DONATE

Gauseva means serving the cows.

Bhagavat Geeta describes how Lord Krishna cared for the cows and calves every morning, by taking them to graze on the Govardhana hill. We should also try to serve the cows with a similar attitude - by providing means for their food, shelter, and medication.

Our mission is to care for stray, abandoned cows, bulls, retired oxen, and orphaned calves. We provide them hay, flour, fresh grass, clean water, medical attention, and a place where they can recuperate from injuries and stay peacefully.
